Our Process

A simple, clear four‑step process to get your institution a professional website. No surprises, no technical jargon.

1

Consultation

We start with a conversation—usually via WhatsApp or a phone call—to understand your institution's needs, goals, and audience.

  • What we discuss: Your institution's purpose, key information to share, target visitors, preferred contact methods.
  • What you get: A clear proposal with scope, timeline, and cost. No obligation to proceed.
  • Time: Usually 30‑60 minutes.
2

Design & Content Setup

We create a design mockup based on your institution's type (school, church, etc.) and work with you to prepare all content.

  • What we do: Design a clean, mobile‑first layout; provide guidance on what content to prepare (text, photos, contacts).
  • What you do: Gather photos, write basic text, confirm contact details.
  • Time: 3‑5 days for design approval and content collection.
3

Development & Review

We build your website using clean HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. You'll review it on a private link and request adjustments.

  • What we do: Code the website, integrate WhatsApp contact, ensure fast loading, test on various devices.
  • What you do: Review the website, test all links and forms, request up to two rounds of changes.
  • Time: 7‑10 days for development and review.
4

Launch & Support

We make your website live, provide hosting, and offer ongoing support for updates and maintenance.

  • What we do: Launch the website, set up hosting, provide basic training, offer maintenance plans.
  • What you get: A fully functional website, hosting for one year, WhatsApp support, clear update process.
  • Time: Launch within 24 hours of final approval.

Common Questions

How long does the entire process take?

Typically 2‑3 weeks from initial consultation to launch, depending on how quickly content is provided.

What do I need to provide?

Basic information about your institution, some photos, contact details, and any existing logos or branding materials.

Can you help with writing content?

Yes, we can provide basic content templates and guidance. For extensive writing, we may recommend a copywriter.

What about training?

We provide simple training on how to request updates and make minor changes. Our goal is to make it easy for non‑technical users.

Ready to Start?

Begin with a simple conversation. We'll explain each step clearly and answer all your questions.
