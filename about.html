Our Focus

KaynAero Technocrats was founded in 2022 to serve Kenyan schools, churches, prayer centres, and small institutions with professional websites that work reliably on the devices and networks available here.

We noticed that many institutions either had no website or had complex, slow websites that didn't work well on low‑end phones. We decided to build simple, fast, mobile‑first websites that focus on clear communication and direct contact.

Serving Kenya since 2022

Our Approach

We believe that a website for an institution should be:

  • Simple: Easy to understand and navigate, especially for non‑technical users.
  • Credible: Presents your institution professionally to build trust.
  • Functional: Works perfectly on all devices, especially mobile phones.
  • Accessible: Loads quickly even on slower internet connections.
  • Maintainable: Easy to update with clear support options.

We use only HTML, CSS, and JavaScript—no complex content management systems that require constant updates and technical knowledge.

Who We Work With

We work directly with principals, pastors, secretaries, administrators, and board members—people who may not be technical but need their institution to have a professional online presence.

Our communication is clear and jargon‑free. We explain options simply, provide straightforward pricing, and focus on what matters: getting your institution visible and credible online.
